Key analyst insights
42.7%
Month-to-month customers churn at 15× the rate of two-year subscribers — contract migration is the single highest-leverage retention action.
47.4%
New customers (0–12 months) churn at nearly half the rate — the first-year onboarding experience is broken and needs targeted intervention.
45.3%
Electronic check users churn at 3× the rate of auto-pay customers — nudging customers toward auto-pay during sign-up could meaningfully reduce churn.
41.9%
Fiber optic customers pay more but churn more — this is a satisfaction problem, not a price problem. Service quality needs investigation.
14.6%
Customers with Online Security churn at less than half the rate — add-on services act as retention anchors, not just revenue streams.
